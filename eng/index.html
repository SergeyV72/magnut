Cereal Bars and Halva

Peanuts, almonds, raisins, honey, seeds, ginger, lemons and oranges are the MagNut cereal bars’ ingredients, which were grown by pure nature. If you enjoy taste and naturalness, then you should read the list of ingredients of MagNut bars! Give them a try and make sure that your hands hold the most delicious source of natural energy.
MagNut cereal bars were tested and got the recommendation as a part of healthy natural diet by Yuri Grinko, the highest category dietician
Yuri Grinko's recommendations, the highest category dietician, on the choice of natural cereal bar:
• Make sure you read the contents of the product on the packaging! Often common «sweet» bar with a lot of artificial ingredients is hidden behind the "healthy" label
• Choose cereal bars that contain less sugar or contain honey. Make sure that there are no cookies, pieces of chocolate or glaze among the ingredients.
• Bars which are recommended as a part of healthy natural diet should not contain artificial sweeteners. Dried fruits and honey should create the taste instead of sugars.
• Choose bars which are made from what you personally know and what you can describe. For example, we all know how dried apricots, almonds, raisins or dates look, but it is hard to imagine what is anhydride dextrose or glycerin in the list of ingredients.
• MagNut cereal bars fully meet all the above criteria and can be recommended as a source of natural energy for everyone who wants to eat deliciously and with benefit to the body. MagNut bars can serve as a full breakfast, an afternoon tea and as well as a perfect snack before and after sports.
• With MagNut bars you not only receive the energy, but, due to 100% naturalness – they help you keep your weight!
